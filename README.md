Superstore Sales Analysis (2014-2017)
Objective:
To analyze sales and profit data from a retail company to identify trends, find opportunities, and improve decision-making.

Tools Used:
• Microsoft Excel
• Pivot Tables
• Charts
• Slicers
• Formulas (TEXT, IF, MONTH, YEAR, etc.)

Steps Taken:
1. Cleaned the data (removed bad values like “a” fixed date columns).
2. Added new columns (Month-Year)
3. Created pivot tables to summarize sales by:
• Month
• Region
• Category
• Segment
4. Built an interactive dashboard with charts and slicers.


KEY INSIGHTS

1. Sales Growth over Time:
Overall sales increased steadily from 2014 to 2017, with the highest annual sales recorded in 2017.
The growth was especially strong in Q4 each year, indicating seasonal spikes in customer
purchasing behavior.

2. Regional Performance:
The West region consistently outperformed other regions in both sales and profit.
Central and South regions generated good sales volumes but showed lower profitability due
higher discounting and lower margins.
3. Category & Sub-Category Insights:
Technology was the most profitable category, driven by high-value products and steady demand.

CONCLUSION

Increase focus on Technology products, especially in the West and East regions.
Evaluate pricing and discount strategy for Office Supplies to improve profit margins.
Explore strategies to improve profitability in the in the Central and South regions, possibly
